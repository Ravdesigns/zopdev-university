§ · who this is for

Architects and platform leads
making decisions
they'll live with for years.

RBAC topology, multi-account credential strategy, showback dimensions, budget pyramids, dashboard governance. The choices a year-1 customer will still respect at year five.

The Architect track is the deepest in the University. It is written for the engineers and FinOps leads who design ZopNight's deployment for their org. Every lesson teaches the trade-off, the alternatives considered, and the reasoning behind ZopNight's choice. No vendor-marketing answers.

You will learn how to design a role model that lets engineers do their jobs without granting more than they need, wire SSO so that diagnosing failures takes under five minutes, walk into a SOC 2 audit with reproducible queries, set up multi-account discovery without managing per-account credentials by hand, build a budget pyramid with proper escalation chains, and design a Sankey investigation that goes from cost question to specific resource in under sixty seconds.
