# Engineering Leader

§ Path · 6 hours total · No cert required (Operator optional)

## Outcome

Enough cloud cost context to ask informed questions of your team and lead cost-aware engineering culture.

## Sequence

```
PHASE 1 — CONTEXT (2 hours)
1. T0.M0.1.L1 — What's in a cloud bill
2. T0.M0.6 — CDCR introduced (all 4)
3. T0.M0.3 — Scheduling beats commitments at non-prod scale (all 4)

PHASE 2 — OWNERSHIP (1.5 hours)
4. T4.M4.1 — FinOps maturity ladder (all 5)
5. T4.M4.4 — Budget governance (all 4)

PHASE 3 — PRODUCT AWARENESS (1.5 hours)
6. T1.M1.3 — Build your first schedule (all 6)
7. T1.M1.4 — Resource groups (all 4)

PHASE 4 — SCALE + RELIABILITY (1 hour)
8. T5.M5.5 — Reliability vs cost (all 4)
9. T5.M5.7 — Cost incident response (all 4)

OPTIONAL: take Operator certification for personal completion.
```

## Why this sequence

- Focus on context + ownership (the things you'll discuss in meetings)
- Light on detailed product mechanics (your team handles those)
- Heavy on the conceptual frameworks (CDCR, maturity, reliability vs cost)
- Cost incident response prepares you for the rare-but-critical moments

## After completion

- Cost-aware leadership
- Can have informed conversations with FinOps + engineering teams
- Ready to lead cost-discipline initiatives
- Optional certification
